ClipGrab

Download and clip videos from X/Twitter, Instagram, Reddit, Vimeo, Twitch, and Streamable.

  1. Download the clipgrab-extension.zip and unzip it.
  2. Open chrome://extensions in your browser.
  3. Enable Developer mode (top-right toggle).
  4. Click Load unpacked and select the unzipped folder.
Format: HH:MM:SS (leave blank for full video)
